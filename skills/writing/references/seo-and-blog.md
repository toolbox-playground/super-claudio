# SEO & Blog Content

## Blog Post Structure That Ranks

### Formula: AIDA + SEO
1. **Hook** (first 100 words) — answer the search intent immediately
2. **Problem** — expand on what the reader is struggling with
3. **Solution** — your main content, structured with H2/H3 subheadings
4. **Proof** — examples, data, case studies
5. **CTA** — what to do next

### SEO Keyword Research (Free Tools)

| Tool | URL | Best For |
|------|-----|----------|
| **Google Search Console** | search.google.com/search-console | Your own site's keywords |
| **Ubersuggest** | neilpatel.com/ubersuggest | Keyword ideas, free tier |
| **AnswerThePublic** | answerthepublic.com | Questions people ask |
| **Google "People Also Ask"** | google.com | Free intent research |
| **Ahrefs Free Tools** | ahrefs.com/free-seo-tools | Keyword difficulty |

### Prompt to Generate a Full Blog Post

Use this with Claude:
```
Write a blog post about [topic] for [audience].
Target keyword: [keyword]
Tone: [professional/casual/educational]
Length: [word count]
Include: introduction, 4-5 H2 sections, conclusion, CTA to [action].
```

### SEO Meta Description Formula

```
[Primary keyword] — [benefit or promise] — [CTA]. Under 160 characters.
```

Example: "AI video tools for TikTok — create product ads in 30 minutes with no filming. Free tools included."

## Content Calendar Template

Weekly rhythm for a brand blog:
- **Monday**: Educational/how-to post (targets informational keywords)
- **Wednesday**: Product/case study post (targets commercial keywords)
- **Friday**: Opinion/trend post (link bait, social shares)

## Repurposing One Blog Post Into 5 Pieces

1. Blog post (full) → base content
2. Instagram carousel (5-7 slides with key points)
3. TikTok/Reels script (60-second talking-head video)
4. X/Twitter thread (10 tweets)
5. Email newsletter (shorter version with CTA)

Ask Claude: "Take this blog post and repurpose it into [format]"
