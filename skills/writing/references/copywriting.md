# Copywriting: Ads, Email & Product Content

## Ad Copy Frameworks

### PAS (Problem → Agitate → Solution)
Best for: Facebook ads, landing pages, email subject lines
```
Problem: [Describe their pain point]
Agitate: [Make the problem feel urgent or worse]
Solution: [Your product/service as the answer]
CTA: [Clear action]
```

### AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action)
Best for: Long-form ads, email sequences, landing pages
```
Attention: [Bold headline that stops them]
Interest: [Relevant fact or story]
Desire: [Benefits + social proof]
Action: [Specific CTA with urgency]
```

### 4U Formula (Useful, Urgent, Unique, Ultra-specific)
Best for: Headlines and subject lines
Example: "Cut your TikTok ad budget in half this week using this free AI tool"
- Useful: saves money
- Urgent: "this week"
- Unique: free AI tool
- Ultra-specific: "TikTok ad budget"

## Email Marketing

### Subject Line Formulas
- **Curiosity:** "You won't believe what this AI can do..."
- **Benefit:** "Make your first TikTok ad in 30 minutes"
- **Question:** "Are you still paying for video editing?"
- **Number:** "3 free tools that replace a $500/month agency"

### Email Structure
```
Subject: [Formula above]
Preview text: [Extends the subject, adds detail]

Body:
- Line 1: Why you're emailing (context)
- Lines 2-4: The value (the thing they care about)
- Lines 5-6: Proof or story
- CTA: One clear button/link
- PS: [Optional second CTA or reinforcement]
```

### Sequence: 5-Email Welcome Series
1. Welcome + quick win (deliver value immediately)
2. Story (your background, why you do this)
3. Problem → solution (their pain + your answer)
4. Social proof (results, testimonials)
5. Offer (your product/service)

## Product Descriptions

### E-commerce Formula
```
[Headline: Main benefit in 1 line]
[Subheadline: For who + outcome]

[3-5 bullet features written as benefits]:
• [Feature] → [what it means for the buyer]
• ...

[Trust element: guarantee, material, certifications]
[CTA: Add to cart / Buy now]
```

Example:
```
Portable Brazilian Coffee Press — Rich espresso anywhere, no electricity needed.
Perfect for travelers and home brewers who refuse to compromise on flavor.

• Double-wall insulation → stays hot for 4 hours
• 30-second brew time → no waiting, no fuss
• Fits in any bag → café quality on flights, camping, the office

30-day money-back guarantee. Ships from São Paulo in 3 days.
[Buy Now — R$89]
```
